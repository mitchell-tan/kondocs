You can change the appearance and color of labels in the organize tabs menu. Open the menu with the "..." on the left

![](/CleanShot2025-03-03at17.08.21.png)

Then click the icon pictures to pick a better one![](/CleanShot2025-03-03at17.09.47.png)

You can change the tab colors on the right![](/CleanShot2025-03-03at17.10.22.png)
