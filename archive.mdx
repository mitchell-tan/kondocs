---
title: "What does archiving do?"
---

When you archive a conversation in Kondo (with shortcut "E"), we move it to a special folder called archive, both in Kondo and in LinkedIn

When you get a new message in a conversation, it will automatically return to your inbox, both in Kondo and LinkedIn

### Where to find the archive in Kondo

2 quick ways to get to the archive in Kondo

1. In the inbox, use shortcut G then E

2. Cmd + K (or Ctrl + K on a PC) then type "Go to Archive"&#x20;
