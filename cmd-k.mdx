---
title: "Command K"
---

Ever wondering "where can I find ...?" or "how do I do ... again?" Try Cmd + K (or Ctrl + K on a PC) and type in what you want, you might just find it there.![](/CleanShot2025-03-03at15.05.51.png)For example, try:

* Cmd + K > Go to Archive

* Cmd + K > Remind Me

* Cmd + K > Clean Up My Inbox

* Cmd + K > Shortcuts
