---
title: "Integrations"
---

Shared integrations are available for customers on our Business or Enterprise plan with \>1 seat. 

To share an integration across your Kondo team, simply click "..." and Share with team.

The integration will run for all users in your Kondo team to the same destination. 

Only Admins can create, update or delete team integrations.
