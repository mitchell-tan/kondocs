---
title: "Team Snippets"
---

You can create team snippets once you have \>1 member on your Kondo team.

Only admins can create or remove team snippets.

Convert existing snippets into team by clicking to "..." on the right of any snippet then "Share with team".

Your snippet will now be available for use instantly for everyone in your team.
