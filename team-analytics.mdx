---
title: "Analytics"
---

Team analytics is available to customers on our Business or Enterprise plan.

You can find the dashboard in Settings -\> Team -\> [Team Analytics](https://app.trykondo.com/settings/analytics).

The dashboard will show stats on

- Messages sent (in Kondo)
- Net new conversations started

For your whole team over the past 4 weeks, broken down to each team member over the past week.
