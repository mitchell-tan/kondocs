---
title: "Team analytics "
---

Team analytics is available **<u>upon request</u>** to customers on our Business or Enterprise plan.

Once enabled, the dashboard will show stats on

- Messages sent (in Kondo)
- Net new conversations started 

For your whole team over the past 4 weeks, broken down to each team member over the past week.

To unlock access to this feature, contact mitchell@trykondo.com
